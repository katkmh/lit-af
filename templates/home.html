Lit: AF

ABOUT

Lit: AF, or literature: a fanclub, is a web application that serves as a literature database for enthusiasts and writers. These include poetries, dramas, proses, etc. You can browse through written pieces from famous to unknown writers or through its title, genre, creation date. These pieces can be rated and reviewed by the users.

Lit: AF aims to cultivate the appreciation for all forms of literature in this generation.
